THALAPATHY VIJAY

actor|katchi tvk|singer

Thalapathy Vijay is one of the most popular and influential actors in the Tamil film industry. Born as Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar on June 22, 1974, he is fondly called "Thalapathy" (commander) by his fans. He began his acting career as a child artist and later rose to stardom through his dedication, charisma, and consistent box office hits. Known for his strong screen presence, energetic dance moves, and powerful performances, Vijay has delivered several blockbuster films like Ghilli, Thuppakki, Mersal, Master, and Leo. Apart from acting, he is admired for his simplicity, philanthropic activities, and the way he connects with his fans. His speeches and roles often carry strong social messages, making him a beloved figure not just in Tamil Nadu but among Tamil-speaking people worldwide.
